Cats are the most close animals to humans,and have lived with people for more than seven thousand years, and cats belong to mammals, and there are many types of cats, which differ in shapes, colors and sizes.

Shirazi cat

The Shirazi cat has long and beautiful fur, and it does not have to be in one color. It can come in any color of the famous cats, and it has a flat face when compared to most other cat breeds.

Himalaya cat

The Himalayan cat is very similar to the Persian, and is considered a subspecies in some cat societies and its own breed in others.

Siamese cat

There are two types of Siamese cats, one with an "apple" shaped head and a plump body, and the other with a large head and slender body.
